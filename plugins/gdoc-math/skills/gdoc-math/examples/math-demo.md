# Math, rendered properly

A quick tour of common formulas — inline like $E = mc^2$ and as display equations.

## Algebra

The quadratic formula solves $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$:

$$x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$$

## A famous identity

Euler's identity ties together five fundamental constants:

$$e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$$

## Sums and series

The sum of the first $n$ natural numbers:

$$\sum_{k=1}^{n} k = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}$$

A geometric series, for $|r| < 1$:

$$\sum_{k=0}^{\infty} r^k = \frac{1}{1 - r}$$

## Calculus

The derivative, from first principles:

$$f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h}$$

The Gaussian integral:

$$\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2}\,dx = \sqrt{\pi}$$

## Geometry

For a right triangle with legs $a$ and $b$ and hypotenuse $c$:

$$a^2 + b^2 = c^2$$
